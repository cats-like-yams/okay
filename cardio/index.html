Cardio Exercises

Cardio exercise, also known as aerobic exercise, is any physical activity that raises your heart rate and increases blood circulation throughout the body.

Benefits

Regular cardio exercise can greatly enhance overall health and well-being. Some of the benefits include:

  • Improved heart and lung health
  • Increased endurance and stamina
  • Enhanced calorie burning for weight management
  • Reduced stress and improved mood
  • Lowered risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and diabetes
  • Boosted immune system function

Exercises and sports

Cardio workouts can be performed in various ways, including running, cycling, swimming, and group fitness classes. Many sports also involve high levels of cardiovascular activity.

Popular cardio-based sports include soccer, basketball, tennis, boxing, and long-distance running.
